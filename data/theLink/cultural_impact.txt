Environmental Message:
The Link continued building Gojira's reputation as environmentally conscious metal artists. The album treats ecological concerns seriously without becoming didactic.

Spiritual Exploration:
The album's spiritual themes exist alongside rather than opposing its environmental concerns, suggesting that ecological consciousness and spiritual awareness are connected.

Indigenous Respect:
By highlighting indigenous wisdom and practices, the album advocated for respect toward cultures often dismissed by Western industrial society.

Consciousness Expansion:
The album's exploration of altered consciousness and expanded awareness reflected growing interest in these topics within progressive metal.
